Design Process

The design process was iterative, starting with hand-drawn sketches to explore spatial relationships and massing. We utilized 3D modeling early on to test solar paths and views, ensuring the final form was both aesthetically pleasing and performant. The client was heavily involved, providing feedback that shaped the material palette and interior flow.
