Kültür / Influencer / İlham Kaynakları

Exploring the foundations of my creative and intellectual process through the architects, movements, and cultural phenomena that shape my work.

Zaha Hadid

"Queen of the curve, who taught me that the parameters of architectural design are boundless."

Bauhaus

"Where functionality meets aesthetics in its purest form, revolutionizing modern design."

Cinematic Vision

"How narrative, light, and atmosphere in films like Blade Runner shape spatial experience."

Mimari Etkiler (Architectural Influencers)

Le Corbusier

A pioneer of modernism, his principles of functionalism and new materials guide my approach to form.

Tadao Ando

A master of light and concrete, his work demonstrates the profound poetry of minimalism.

Lina Bo Bardi

Her designs emphasize social engagement and cultural context, inspiring a human-centric approach.

Frank Lloyd Wright

His philosophy of organic architecture and harmony with nature is a constant source of inspiration.

Kültürel Esinlenmeler (Cultural Inspirations)

Wabi-Sabi

The Japanese aesthetic of finding beauty in imperfection and impermanence, influencing material choice and texture.

Andrei Tarkovsky

His films' long takes and poetic use of natural landscapes inform my approach to atmosphere and time in space.

Sanat/Tasarım Akımları (Art/Design Movements)

De Stijl

Pure abstraction and universality by a reduction to the essentials of form and colour.

Brutalism

Emphasis on materials, texture, and construction, producing powerfully expressive forms.

Metabolism

A post-war Japanese movement that fused ideas about architectural megastructures with organic biological growth.
