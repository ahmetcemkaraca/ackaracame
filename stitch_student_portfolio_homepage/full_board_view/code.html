Semester Project, Fall 2023

Urban Oasis Project

The Urban Oasis Project re-imagines urban living through the integration of sustainable architecture and community-focused design. The primary objective was to create a mixed-use development that not only provides housing but also fosters a sense of community and connection with nature within a dense city environment.

Our methodology involved parametric design tools to optimize natural light and ventilation, combined with the use of locally-sourced, sustainable materials. My specific contributions included the initial conceptual design, 3D modeling in Rhino, and producing the final renderings using V-Ray, focusing on creating realistic depictions of the building's interaction with its surroundings.
